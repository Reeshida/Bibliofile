Build your collection:

✒︎ Bibliofile allows you to create your own library catalog, by adding books you already own to your virtual library.

✒︎ You may also create lists of books you wish to sell, and books you would like to buy.

✒︎ Set your minimum price to sell, and max price to buy. You will be matched with other users who fit that range, and then you may commence negotiations via email.
